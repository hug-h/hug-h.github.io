Research Project 2024/2025
Carried out in fulfillment of a BSc in Music Media & Performance Technology at the University of Limerick


This research investigates the Buchla 250e Dual Arbitrary Function Generator and presents a series of max patches which encapsulate the conceptual metaphors of the 250e in a new textually controlled environment, grounded in live performance contexts.
