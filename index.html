Hugh Clery Ward

Music Media & Performance Technology Student

Bio // Work

Limerick, Ireland



Hugh is a Musician and Technologist from Limerick City, working with code, sound, and visual mediums, and currently in the final year of a Bachelor's Degree in Music Media and Performance Technology at the University of Limerick.

Coming from a background as a percussionist he focuses on digital processes, working primarily with Max MSP to create tools, performance systems and compositions rooted in the post-digital aesthetic. Aside from his solo practice, Hugh plays with a number of bands and groups in Limerick City, currently: Martian Subculture and John Foley, The Prize Band
