Limerick, Ireland



Musician and media person from Limerick City, working with code and sound.

Working in Max-MSP to create musical tools, performance systems and compositions. Hugh has a background as a percussionist and studied a bachelor's degree in Music Media and Performance Technology at the University of Limerick.
Alongside solo practice, Hugh plays drums with John Foley, The Prize Band and Martian Subculture
