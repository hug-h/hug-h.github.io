Work // Bio

Hugh is a Musician and Technologist from Limerick City, working with code, sound, and visual mediums, and currently in the final year of a Bachelor's Degree in Music Media and Performance Technology at the University of Limerick.

Coming from a background as a percussionist and street performer, he focuses on digital processes, working primarily with Max MSP to create tools, patches and improvisations rooted in the post-digital aesthetic. Aside from his solo practice, Hugh plays with a number of bands and groups in Limerick City, currently: Martian Subculture and John Foley's Prize Band
