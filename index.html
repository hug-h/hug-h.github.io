Hugh Clery Ward

Music Media & Performance Technology Student

Bio // Work

Limerick, Ireland



Musician and media person from Limerick City, working primarily with code and sound. Currently in the final year of a Bachelor's Degree in Music Media and Performance Technology at the University of Limerick.

With a background as a percussionist, now focusing on digital processes, working primarily with Max-MSP to create tools, performance systems and compositions rooted in the post-digital aesthetic. Alongside solo practice, Hugh plays with a number of bands and groups in Limerick City, currently: Martian Subculture and John Foley, The Prize Band
