RD2DOOM

Music Video 2024
Shoot, Edit & Concept
2m19s

Utilising a mixture of hyperlapse photography, video, and 3D animation, this short fast paced video takes advantage of flash photography and low resolution 3D scans to create an overexposed, jarring aesthetic, heavily inspired by You Wouldn't Steal a Car

No-budget music video for the track RD2DOOM by Monplaisir
Solo shoot using android phone camera & Nikon SB20 flash in and around the University of Limerick in May 2024

Development began with 3D scans in polycam prompting the juxtaposition of 3D renders and photography while maintaining the same pattern of motion around the chair as it moves through urban landsacpes.
